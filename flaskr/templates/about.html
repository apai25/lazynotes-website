About

LazyNotes effectively summarizes sources into a collection of important details. In order to accommodate various formats, LazyNotes was created to be able to summarize audio as well as text. For example, if a student wants to record a lecture, they can upload a recording to download a summary. Additionally, pictures can be uploaded to LazyNotes directly, instead of text, as our app has the capabilities to detect text from pictures.
